Morphological description
This tissue shows a diffuse monotonous infiltrate with small lymphoid cells with slightly irregular nuclei. Small germinal centres are seen within the lymphoid infiltrate. The cells have clear cytoplasm. Two lymphoepithelial lesions are seen. Normal salivary gland is not present.
Immunophenotype

Immunophenotype of lymphoid cells:
CD20/PAX5 highlight a nodular B- cell infiltrate with focally, small CD10+, BCL2-, BCL6+ germinal centres. The expanded mantle/marginal zone is CD5-, CCND1-, IGK(equivocal), IGL(equivocal). There is an important internodular CD3+/CD5+ T cell infiltrate. The germinal centres are supported by CD21+ follicular dendritic cells.
