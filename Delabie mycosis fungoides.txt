Diagnosis

Morphological description
The skin shows epidermis and superficial dermis. The epidermis shows moderate acanthosis, focal parakeratosis. Spongiosis or interface pathology is not prominent. Epidermal Pautrier abscesses are present, consisting of dendritic cells and small lymphoid cells and intermediate cells with irregular nuclei. Single lymphoid cells infiltration is also seen as well as focal linear arrangements of lymphoid cells at the basal membrane. There is prominent follicular mucinosis, and also prominent lymphocytic lymphotropism. Very focal interface pathology is also seen.
The pars papillaris of the dermis shows perivascular lymphoid cell and dendritic cell infiltration.
