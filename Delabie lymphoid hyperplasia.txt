Diagnosis
Needle biopsy of abdominopelvic lymph node: lymphoid hyperplasia
Microscopic Description
This lymph node shows a preserved architecture. The cortex is hypoplastic with several primary follicles and an atretic follicle. The paracortex is not expanded. Sinuses are preserved and medullary tracts show plasma cells and small lymphoid cells. Atypical cells are not seen.
